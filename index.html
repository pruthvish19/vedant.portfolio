Vedant qualified as a Chartered Accountant through ICAI, clearing CA Foundation, both groups of Intermediate, and both groups of Finals — the last completed in September 2025 with an exemption in one subject.

He's since moved to EY, carrying that grounding in compliance and controls into a larger stage. Off the ledger, he's just as comfortable holding a room — through cricket, mimicry, and stand-up comedy.
